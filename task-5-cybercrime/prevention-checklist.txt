Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify payment requests before accepting
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication on accounts
- Regularly check your bank transactions
- Use only trusted apps for payments
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
