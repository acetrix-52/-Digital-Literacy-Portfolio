UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. It usually happens when a scammer contacts a person through a phone call, message, or social media pretending to be from a bank, payment service, or customer support.

The scammer tricks the victim by sending a fake payment link or asking them to approve a payment request. Sometimes, they ask for sensitive information like OTP or PIN. Once the victim follows these instructions, money is instantly transferred from their bank account.

Students and elderly people are often targeted because they may not be fully aware of such scams. These frauds can lead to serious financial loss and stress for the victim.

In many cases, the money is difficult to recover if not reported immediately. This shows how important it is to stay alert and avoid trusting unknown calls or messages related to money.
